# Instructor Agreement (Template)

> **Status.** Template.  The project owner may customise per
> deployment; this version is the default the
> `academics@packetfive.com` request flow points at.

I, the undersigned, request access to the
`PacketFive/MERLIN-V-solutions` private repository for the
purpose of teaching a course based on the MERLIN-V academic
materials.  In exchange for read access, I agree to the
following:

## 1. Do not redistribute

I will not publish, post, fork-to-public, or otherwise share
the contents of the solutions repository.  This includes:

- Not committing the contents to a public repository.
- Not posting solutions on a course web site accessible to
  students.
- Not emailing solutions to students directly.
- Not including solutions in handouts or printed materials.

## 2. Do not distribute to students

The solutions exist for instructor reference and for use in
end-of-term release after assessment is complete (if the
instructor chooses to do so).  Mid-course distribution is out
of scope.

## 3. Treat hidden test cases as confidential

If I am granted access to the third-tier private repository
containing hidden test cases (typically only project-owner
access), I will not share those test cases or their expected
outputs by any channel.

## 4. Report compromise

If I have reason to believe the solutions have been
inadvertently exposed (e.g. I accidentally forked to a public
repo and reverted), I will notify
`academics@packetfive.com` immediately so the owner can
assess and respond.

## 5. Right to revoke

Access can be revoked at any time at the project owner's
discretion, with or without cause.
